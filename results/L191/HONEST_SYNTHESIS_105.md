>    parameterization* rather than a unique inference.
>
> 2. **Landau-Ginzburg mechanism**: The proposed V(n; ρ_m) form
>    (Section 3.3) is a phenomenological ansatz. Multiple
>    microscopic origins are possible (condensed matter analogy,
>    Coleman-Weinberg, symmetry breaking) but none are yet derived.
>
> 3. **RG fixed points**: The cubic β-function (Section 3.4)
>    requires specific coefficients that are not derived from
>    first-principles loop calculations. Real one-loop calculations
>    yield only two fixed points.

>    (self-consistency, Bunch-Davies, holographic) but is a
>    *postulate* about the cosmic-scale quantum lifetime rather
>    than a derived consequence.
>
> The framework's strength lies in its *predictive content* (14
> falsifiable predictions, 11 already verified) rather than its
> theoretical depth at this stage. Future work and observational
