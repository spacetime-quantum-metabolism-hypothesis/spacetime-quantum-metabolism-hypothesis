## Abstract draft

We propose Spacetime Quantum Theory (SQT), a unified framework deriving dark energy and modified gravity from a single metabolism field n(x,t) governed by 6 axioms (a1-a6) and 5 derived relations (D1-D5). The Branch B 3-regime σ_0(z) parametrisation passes 14/14 dimensional and self-consistency tests, predicts ρ_q/ρ_Λ = 1.0000 via Bianchi balance, and yields ΔAICc = 99 over LCDM on DESI DR2 13-point BAO + DESY5 SN + Planck CMB compressed (with anchor caveat). Five out-of-sample predictions (P15-P19: GW scalar mode, 21cm T_b, DESI DR3 scaling, redshift drift, Euclid S_8) provide near-term falsifiability via LIGO O5, SKA, DESI, ELT-HIRES, and Euclid. Limitations: σ_8 tension worsens by +1.14% under dark-only embedding; H0 tension unresolved; tau_q derived as 1/(n0 σ_0) = t_Pl from a3 (no free parameter).
