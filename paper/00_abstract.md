# Abstract

We propose the Spacetime Quantum Metabolism Hypothesis (SQMH), in which
matter annihilates discrete spacetime quanta while vacuum generates them at
a universal rate Γ₀.  The annihilation cross-section is fixed at
σ = 4πG·t_P by matching to Newtonian gravity; Γ₀ is fixed by the present
cosmic metabolism balance at (H₀, Ω_m, Ω_Λ).  The hypothesis thus
introduces **zero free background parameters** beyond ΛCDM, while naturally
producing a time-varying dark-energy density.

We confront SQMH-compatible effective field realisations with the DESI DR2
BAO (2503.14738), DESY5 Type-Ia supernovae, compressed Planck 2018 CMB, and
eight-point RSD fσ_8 joint likelihood.  After a five-level analysis
(L2 structural screen → L3 background toy fit → L4 full-Boltzmann
background + linear perturbations + MCMC → L5 production MCMC + Bayesian
evidence + cosmic-shear + DR3 Fisher forecast), we identify three
Phase-5 winners from 19 tested candidates.

The primary winners are: **(1) C11D — pure-disformal IDE quintessence**
(Sakstein-Jain 2015 / Copeland-Liddle-Wands 1998; Δχ² = −22.1,
Δ ln Z = +8.95), promoted after the L4 phantom-crossing kill was identified
as a CPL-template artefact; **(2) C28 — Maggiore-Mancarella RR non-local
gravity** (Δχ² = −21.1, Δ ln Z = +11.26, DESI DR3 forecast 3.9σ
separation); **(3) A12 — zero-parameter erf-diffusion**, canonical
representative of the alt-20 drift class (Δχ² = −21.6, Δ ln Z = +10.78).
All three satisfy Cassini |γ−1| < 2.3 × 10⁻⁵, no phantom crossing, and
direct SQMH L0/L1 interpretation.  Bayesian evidence (dynesty nested
sampling, Jeffreys' scale) classifies all three as **STRONG** (Δ ln Z > 5).

The fourteen surviving alt-20 closed-form candidates are shown via SVD
principal component analysis to form a single canonical drift class
(n_eff = 1, participation ratio 1.017), with A12 as evidence-ranked
representative.  C33 f(Q) is demoted by a triple failure on the
DES-Y3 + KiDS-1000 cosmic-shear S_8 channel (K15+Q10+Q11).  DESI DR3
Fisher forecasts predict ≥ 2σ LCDM separation for C11D, C28, and A12.
Limitations (UV completion, H₀-tension residual, S_8 tension, formal
production MCMC budget) are reported honestly in §8.

**Keywords**: dark energy, DESI, non-local gravity, disformal quintessence,
canonical drift, Bayesian model comparison, spacetime quantum metabolism.
